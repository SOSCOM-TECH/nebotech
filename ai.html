Generative AI for Swahili Language

At NEBO TECH AI Department, we are committed to advancing the use of Generative AI (GenAI) for Swahili, one of the most widely spoken languages in Africa.

  • Chatbots and Virtual Assistants: Designing conversational AI solutions tailored to Swahili speakers for businesses and community services.
  • Swahili Language Models: Developing advanced natural language generation models to produce high-quality, context-aware Swahili text.
  • Translation Systems: Building tools to seamlessly translate Swahili to other languages and vice versa, promoting inclusivity in global communications.
  • Content Creation:Enabling automated generation of Swahili content, such as articles, marketing copy, and educational materials

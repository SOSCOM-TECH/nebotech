Job Opportunity

Cybersecurity Engineer

Full-Time Position

We are seeking a talented Cybersecurity Engineer to join our team. If you're passionate about protecting systems and data, and have hands-on experience in the field of cybersecurity, this is the perfect opportunity for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor, investigate, and respond to security events and alerts to mitigate risks.
  • Develop and maintain anti-malware solutions for various environments.
  • Implement and manage security controls aligned with ISO 27001 standards.
  • Conduct risk assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Perform penetration testing on new and existing systems using both automated and manual techniques.
  • Provide guidance on security design, verify security requirements, and test systems to ensure security standards are met.
  • Develop and implement advanced security test plans, scenarios, and scripts.
  • Produce detailed cybersecurity reports for stakeholders.
  • Organize and deliver technical and non-technical cybersecurity training sessions.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest cybersecurity trends, threats, and countermeasures.

Minimum Requirements

  • Minimum of 3 years experience as a Systems Administrator, Systems Engineer, or Network Engineer.
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in ICT-related fields (Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Cybersecurity, etc).
  • Experience with Azure, AWS, MSCE, CCNA, Security+, CEH, CISA, CISM, CISSP, or OSCP.
  • Exposure to industry standards: ISO27001, NIST, CIS, Mitre Attack, OWASP.
  • Background in incident response plans (IRP) and disaster recovery (DR).
  • Contactable references required.

Salary and Application Process

Salary: Negotiable based on experience

How to Apply: Send your CV to info@nebo.co.tz

Deadline: Applications due by January 10, 2025
